Popular Men's Fashion Style

Casual Wear

Less formal, relaxed, focused on comfort, style and practicality. The casual fashion style is perfect to go out, to visit friends, or for everyday use. Casual wear usually consist of confortable individual article of clothing such as t-shirts, jeans, and sneakers.

Formal Wear

Every man should have at least one formal wear in their wardrobe. As its name implies, formal wear is used for formal events, such as meetings, work, weddings, and more. An example would be tuxedos, white shirts, and suits.

Street Wear

A subsection of casual clothing, streetwear focuses on comfort and trendiness. It was popularize in the 90s and portrays youth and urban subcultures. An example of street wear would be graphic shirts, beanies, hoodies, baseball caps, trendy sneakers, and more.

Biker Wear

Inspired by rock-n-roll and as its name implies, biker fashion traditionally consists of black-on-black outfits and accessories that goes very well with large motorcycles. An example would be jeans, leather jacket, and sunglasses.
