Our Origin

Meet Jamal.

In 2010, Jamal was just 22 after he just graduated university with a degree in finance when he founded M&T. It all started with his interest in clothing and the fashion industry. He noticed that "fashion" is often associated with women and sees the lack of male presence in the fashion industry.

After conducting an extensive amount of research, he concluded that most men are oblivious when it comes to fashion and cares less about clothing compared to women. This however, is caused by lots of factors, one of them being a lack of choice in clothing and representation of men's fashion.

Upon realizing this, not long after, he decided to produce male focused clothing and experiment with fashion styles to give men more options to go through. Thus, M&T was born.

Our History

After opening the first M&T store in Jakarta, Indonesia in 2011, it wasn't an instant hit and Jamal had to look for ways to promote his business to reach more ears. Luckily, he had a friend working in fashion design, and advertisment.

Deciding to work together, his business now seems to attract more and more customers. Old customer keep coming seeking quality item, along with new customers trying out M&T's product. After their first success, they decided to open 2 more branches. One in Surabaya, and another one in Medan.

Fast forward to 2022, M&T is known as men's favourite place to shop for clothing, and has branches all around the globe, to name a few: New York, Tokyo, and Queensland. Till this day, M&T is still expanding and is experimenting and developing more and more products for its consumers.
